About Us

The company was founded because we believe that software should make the lives of business owners easier, not harder.

Building and scaling great software can be hard, even for specialists. It shouldn't be for you.

Based on our experience we found that very often businesses were so focused on their core business they didn't have enough time to spend on software.

All too often an off the shelf or outsourced solution was chosen so they could get back to running their business.

Off the shelf assumes that the solution that's being sold matches your problem well. The sale is agreed on the hopes that it can and will continue to. This is all well and good until it doesn't.

Outsourcing has the allure of being hands off. Since the dominant pricing model is time based, it means you can't really be as hands off as you'd like to be.

We felt there's got to be a better way to do this. So much so that we left the comfort of our jobs to give it a try.

We've been around since 2019 and our team has grown from two to three. The team is based in Ireland at the moment and have embraced remote working.

Perhaps you'll join us on this journey.
